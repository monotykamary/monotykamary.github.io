## Traits

**Confer Fire Resistance**: The nightmare can grant [resistance](https://roll20.net/compendium/dnd5e/Rules:Combat?expansion=0#toc_51) to [fire](https://roll20.net/compendium/dnd5e/Rules:Combat?expansion=0#toc_50) damage to anyone riding it.  
  
**Illumination**: The nightmare sheds bright light in a 10-foot radius and [dim light](https://roll20.net/compendium/dnd5e/Rules:The%20Environment?expansion=0#toc_3) for an additional 10 feet.  
  

## Actions

**Hooves**: Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 13 (2d8 + 4) bludgeoning damage plus 7 (2d6) fire damage.  
  
**Ethereal Stride**: The nightmare and up to three willing creatures within 5 feet of it magically enter the [Ethereal Plane](https://roll20.net/compendium/dnd5e/Lore:The%20Planes%20of%20Existence?expansion=0#toc_4) from the [Material Plane](https://roll20.net/compendium/dnd5e/Lore:The%20Planes%20of%20Existence?expansion=0#toc_1), or vice versa.
